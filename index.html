Troop 471

About the Troop

Troops 471B and 471G are part of the Scout BSA Alamo Area Council. We offer a well-rounded scouting experience with an emphasis on outdoor activities, rank advancement, and service. The troops serve the Rio Medina area, which is conveniently located near Castroville and Far West San Antonio.
